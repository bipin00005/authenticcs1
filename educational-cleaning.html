Educational Facility Cleaning Services

At Authentic Commercial Services, we understand that clean and healthy learning environments are essential for student success. Our specialized educational facility cleaning services are designed to maintain the highest standards of cleanliness and hygiene in schools, colleges, and other educational institutions.

Our Educational Cleaning Services Include:

  • Classroom Cleaning

    Thorough cleaning and sanitization of classrooms, desks, and learning equipment

  • Common Area Maintenance

    Regular cleaning of hallways, cafeterias, and recreational spaces

  • Restroom Sanitization

    Comprehensive cleaning and disinfection of all restroom facilities

  • Laboratory Cleaning

    Specialized cleaning for science labs and technical facilities

  • Sports Facility Maintenance

  • Library & Study Areas

    Careful cleaning of library spaces and study areas
