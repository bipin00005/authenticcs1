Specialized Cleaning for Educational Facilities

At Authentic Commercial Services, we understand that maintaining a clean, safe, and healthy environment is crucial for educational and childcare facilities. Our specialized cleaning services are designed to meet the unique needs of schools, daycare centers, and educational institutions.

Our Education & Childcare Cleaning Services Include:

  • Classroom & Play Area Sanitization

    Thorough cleaning and disinfection of learning spaces and play equipment.

  • Child-Safe Cleaning Products

    Use of non-toxic, environmentally friendly cleaning solutions.

  • High-Touch Surface Disinfection

    Regular sanitization of frequently touched surfaces and shared equipment.

  • After-Hours Cleaning

    Flexible scheduling to minimize disruption to educational activities.
