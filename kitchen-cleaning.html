Professional Kitchen Cleaning Services

At Authentic Commercial Services, we understand the critical importance of maintaining a clean and hygienic commercial kitchen environment. Our specialized kitchen cleaning services are designed to meet the highest standards of cleanliness and comply with all relevant health and safety regulations.

Our Kitchen Cleaning Services Include:

  • Deep Cleaning of Equipment

    Thorough cleaning of ovens, grills, fryers, and all kitchen equipment

  • Hood and Exhaust System Cleaning

    Complete cleaning of ventilation systems and exhaust hoods

  • Floor and Surface Sanitization

    Deep cleaning and sanitization of all kitchen surfaces and floors

  • Grease Trap Maintenance

    Regular cleaning and maintenance of grease traps

  • Food Storage Area Cleaning

    Thorough cleaning of refrigerators, freezers, and storage areas

  • Waste Management

    Proper cleaning and sanitization of waste disposal areas
